This season, our Limited Edition Winter collection is inspired by folklore and forest magic, as we delve into an intriguing & fantastical woodlands.

Two new fragrances will take you on a mystical journey – Into the Woods.

Showcasing the best traits of their sophisticated woody fragrance family (think complex and lingering) two Triple Scented Candles make a fairytale winter duo.

Woodland Wander, opens with warm and sweet spices then deepens to reveal rare woods (Rosewood, Sandalwood), made dewy and glistening with the sparkle of ginger.
While, the smouldering Sacred Hollow, is a bold and exotic tribute to treasured ingredients. Oud, Frankincense and Noble Resins combine in this rich and complex offering.

Packaged beautifully, the box artwork for the collection uses a 3D effect illustration, as though it is drawing you deeper into this fabled land.
The vessels are adorned with intricate patterns of rambling woods and the wild forest creatures hidden within.
Adding to the intrigue, they cast shadows that seem to dance as the flame flickers from behind.

Ready to connect with these majestic woods and what lies within?
